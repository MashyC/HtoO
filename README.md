## What it does
H to O is a comprehensive app, which allows people to find the essential supplies closest to them. When you open the app you are presented with the Maps Page which shows you a map with the stores around you. When you tab a store it shows you the stores inventory and clicking this popup shows the directions to the store. The Shopping List page allows you to add the items you require and when you click confirm it takes you to the map page and shows you the store closest to you and with the items you need.
